Letters to the Editors

"Realism"

Clarence Thurman from Kentucky City, Massachusetts, is an awful person and should not buy any more of our issues. Also, Donovan is colored:

“Realism! That’s the name of the game, and that’s why I’m writing. I compliment you all, fine sirs, on your realism in regard to the races. In every issue, the proud white-skinned protaganists triumph over the lesser races (fictional though they may be). There are a few hiccups, and a few too many instances of the “noble savage” that cavorts with the more civilized of characters, but in the last issue, in “Tales to Pacfici,” it was clearly Donovan’s shining wit that saved the day against those mutated and deranged brutes (perhaps an allegory?). Hat (and hood!) off to you, sirs!”
